About MITSCounsel

The purpose of clinical counseling is that students can freely and confidentially express their academic, emotional and personal pressures and concerns to a professional who can help them effectively.

The major objective of the counseling centre is to facilitate Academic, Emotional, Social and cognitive development of the students hence to empower them in their learning and personal development.Other than students, parents and teachers are also getting the benefits from the counsellor in order to pave a path to the students inside the campus and in their homes. Evening sessions are mainly conducting for Hostel inmates who are away from their own homes.
